House of Vision Only For You

House of Vision currently has two stores located on UBC and SFU campus. Our UBC store has been serving the campus community for over 15 years and remains active in many community organized events. We believe it is important to better the community as it is an essential part of our business.

In spring 2018, we opened our second location in the SFU Burnaby campus due to the increasing demand. We urge to be an active part of the vibrant SFU neighborhood.

From designer, high-end fashion frames and sunglasses to practical eyewear, we are here to meet all your optical needs.
