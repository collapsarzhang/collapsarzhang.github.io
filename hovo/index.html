House of Vision

House of Vision has two locations at the heart of the University of British Columbia and Simon Fraser University. Our UBC Optical has been serving the community for over 15 years and remains active in many events helping the community. Our philosophy is to improve quality of life and we are proud of the outcome we have seen for the past years.

Due to our great success and positive feedback from our clients, patients and local community, we were in high demand to continue our legacy and in March of 2018, we opened our optical on the Simon Fraser campus.

From designer and high-end fashion frames and sunglasses to practical eyewear, we are here to meet all your vision needs.

Services For Students, Staff & the Public

House of Vision Optical provides full comprehensive eye exams, glasses, sunglasses and contact lens fitting.

Eye Exams – ­ We have fully licensed doctors of optometry on site. Our highly experienced doctors provide primary care and comprehensive eye examinations for students, adults and children. Services include management of ocular diseases, Emergency eye care, and laser correction co-management with Ophthalmologists.

Eye Glasses -­ We carry over 1,000 designer frames in-­store including Christian Dior, Tom Ford, Bvlgari, Thom Browne, Lindberg, Cartier, Gucci, Saint Laurent, Ray-­Ban and more!

We also offer affordable high quality frames and lens packages starting at $100.

Standard of Care – ­Various services we provide are covered by your medical services plan (MSP) or third party insurance, so make an appointment today!

Insurance & Direct billing -­ All full-­time students, staff and faculty at our locations are covered by a health plan. We are experts on your vision care coverage and would be more than happy to explain your benefits.

Most importantly, We are able to direct-­bill and/or direct-­submit to many insurance carriers - ­just ask us for details!
