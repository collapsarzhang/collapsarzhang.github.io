House of Vision Only For You

House of Vision currently has two stores located on UBC and SFU campus. Our UBC store has been serving the campus community for over 15 years and remains active in many community organized events. We believe it is important to better the community as it is an essential part of our business.

In spring 2018, we opened our second location in the SFU Burnaby campus due to the increasing demand. We urge to be an active part of the vibrant SFU neighborhood.

From designer, high-end fashion frames and sunglasses to practical eyewear, we are here to meet all your optical needs.

Services For Students, Staff & the Public

House of Vision Optical provides comprehensive eye exams, glasses, sunglasses and contact lenses.

Eye Exams – ­ Two full-­time optometrists are on site. All our exams are performed by licensed doctors of optometry. We provide best service to everyone including students, staff and faculty and the general public.

Eye Glasses -­ We carry over 1,000 designer frames in-­store including Christian Dior, Tom Ford, Bvlgari, Tiffany&Co., Fendi, Gucci, Giorgio Armani, Burberry, Ray-­Ban and more!

We also offer affordable frame and lens packages starting at $100.

Eye health concern – ­Many services are covered by your provincial health plan or private insurance, so make an appointment today!

Insurance & Direct billing -­ All full-­time students, staff and faculty at our locations are covered by a health plan. We are experts on your vision care coverage and would be more than happy to explain further.

Most importantly, We are able to direct-­bill and/or direct-­submit to many insurance carriers ­ ask us for details!
